Cancellation and Refund

Effective Date: 12/04/2023

We appreciate your interest in the Limited Capital Trading Challenge organized by Limited Capital Trading Challenge. Before registering for the challenge, we kindly ask you to review our cancellation and refund policy outlined below.

Cancellation Policy:

Once a participant has successfully registered for the Limited Capital Trading Challenge, the registration is considered final, and cancellations are not permitted. We do not entertain requests for cancellation, and participants are committed to participating in the challenge for the entire duration specified during the registration process.

Refund Policy:

Limited Capital Trading Challenge has a strict no-refund policy. Once a participant completes the registration process and submits the required entry fee, the payment is considered non-refundable under any circumstances.

Changes to the Policy:

Limited Capital Trading Challenge reserves the right to modify or amend this cancellation and refund policy at any time. Participants are encouraged to review this policy periodically for updates.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this cancellation and refund policy, please contact us.

By completing the registration process for the Limited Capital Trading Challenge, participants acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agreed to abide by the terms of this cancellation and refund policy.

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
