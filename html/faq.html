Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is the Limited Capital Trading Challenge?

The Limited Capital Trading Challenge is a trading competition organized by The Limited Capital Trading Challenge. Participants trade with their own capital within a specified time frame, aiming to achieve the highest returns.

2. How do I participate?

To participate, you need to register for the challenge by completing the registration process on our website. Follow the instructions provided, including the submission of the entry fee.

3. What is the entry fee, and is it refundable?

The entry fee is different for different challenges, and it is non-refundable once paid. Please refer to our Cancellation and Refund for more details.

4. How are winners determined?

Winners are determined based on the highest percentage returns achieved during the specified challenge duration. Detailed criteria for determining winners changes for every challenge. Please read the rules for the particular challenge before participating.

5. Can I trade with any broker/platform?

For now, participants needs to have a Zerodha account to participant in our challenges.

6. How do I stay updated on challenge information?

Stay informed by regularly checking our website and following us on out social media platforms for the latest announcements, updates, and challenge information.

7. If I lose a challenge, is there a chance for a refund?

No, we do not provide any refund if the participant losses a challenge.

If you have additional questions not addressed here, feel free to contact us.
