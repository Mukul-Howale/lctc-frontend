Rules

These rules are put in place to ensure a fair and transparent trading challenge for all participants. Violation of any rules may result in disqualification or other appropriate actions determined by Limited Capital Trading Challenge.

General Rules

Prize Distribution:

A total of 50 winners will be selected based on their trading performance, with the 1st prize awarded to the participant achieving the highest returns. Subsequent prizes will decrease in value. The final decision in selecting the winners rests with us, and no changes will be made based on external requests or appeals.

Winners Announcement:

The announcement of winners will take place two weeks after the conclusion of the challenge. Winners will be contacted via their registered email addresses with instructions on how to collect their respective prizes.

Platform Requirement:

Participants must use Zerodha as their trading platform for the challenge. Any trades conducted on platforms other than Zerodha will not be considered valid for the competition.

Eligible Trading Instruments:

Participants are limited to trading in Bank Nifty, Nifty, and Fin Nifty options only. Trades made with other financial instruments will result in disqualification.

Capital Limit:

Each participant must start with a maximum capital of 20,000 INR. Trades conducted with an amount exceeding the assigned capital will lead to automatic disqualification from the challenge.

Objective:

Participants are required to maximize returns within the given time frame to secure a prize. The challenge aims to assess trading acumen and strategic decision-making.

Submission Rules

Submission Deadline:

Participants must submit both their tradebook XLSX file and the verified P&L (Profit and Loss) link on 20/01/2024 before 11:00 PM (noon). Submissions received after the stipulated time will not be accepted under any circumstances.

Unaltered Tradebook:

The tradebook file submitted must remain unchanged and untampered. Any alterations to the file will result in immediate disqualification from the challenge. Participants are advised to review their submissions carefully before the deadline.

Verification Process:

The P&L link provided by participants must be accessible and verifiable. Any discrepancies or issues with verification may lead to disqualification. Participants should ensure the accuracy and integrity of their submissions.
